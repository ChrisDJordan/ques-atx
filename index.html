About Us

Your local Cable Park

Quest ATX is Austin's first and only cable wakepark with big ambition to become a complete action sports destination. Set on 130 uniquely-manicured acres, and located close to the new Circuit of the Americas, Quest ATX is a world class wakeboarding facility. The park is equipped with a six-tower Sesitec system, UNIT Parktec rails and a System 2.0. (perfect for beginners!) Pairing Austin’s ‘Keep It Weird’ vibe with a of land of endless possibilities, Quest ATX is set to be one of the most unique cable wakeboard parks in the United States.

What is cable wakeboarding?

Cable wakeboarding is simply wakeboarding while being pulled not by boat, but by an over-head cableski system. It’sdefinitely the coolest addition to the distinguished list of extreme sports throughout the world, because it com- binesthe best of the extreme nature of wake-boarding without the need for(or expense of) aboat. Cable is an enormously valuable and important element of the entire sport of wakeboarding.

Programs

Camps

Quest ATX offers a summer water sports camp for children ages 6 to 16. At this unique camp, your son or daughter will learn to wakeboard, kneeboard and water-ski in a fun, supportive environment from our experienced and professional staff.

LEARN MORE

Lessons

Lessons are the perfect way to kick start your day of fun in the sun at Quest! Our private coaches are professional and a lot of fun. Whether you are learning to ride for the first time or want to take your riding skills to the next level, our staff can help you.

LEARN MORE

Parties

Quest ATX will provide you and your team with an exciting and unforgettable teambuilding experience. We have customized event planning or create your own park experience for up to 150 people. We offer catering so that your team can relax and fully enjoy their outdoor experiences in our beautiful Austin countryside setting. No experience is necessary.
