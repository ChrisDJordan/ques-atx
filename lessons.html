Lessons at Quest

Lessons are the perfect way to kick start your day of fun in the sun at Quest! Our private coaches are professional and a lot of fun. Whether you are learning to ride for the first time or want to take your riding skills to the next level, our staff can help you. Click here to schedule lessons online. More information on pricing and what each package includes is below.

Private Lessons

Our private cable lessons are designed to teach the fundamentals and improve your skills from there. We will break down your riding form and correct any bad habits you may have. You will then have the knowledge and confidence to try anything! If you are more advanced rider we will partner you with a coach that can teach specific tricks you are looking to learn.
